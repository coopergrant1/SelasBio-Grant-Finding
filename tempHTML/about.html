About Grant Finder

Revolutionizing grant discovery through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

Grant Finder is designed to bridge the gap between innovative research projects and the funding opportunities they need to succeed. We understand that finding the right grants can be overwhelming, time-consuming, and often feels like searching for a needle in a haystack.

Our AI-powered platform leverages advanced natural language processing through Claude 3 to analyze thousands of grant opportunities and match them precisely to your research goals, methodology, and institutional requirements.

How It Works

Smart Search

Enter your research keywords and we'll fetch the most relevant grants from Grants.gov's comprehensive database in real-time.

AI Analysis

Claude 3 analyzes your project description against available opportunities, understanding context and nuance beyond simple keyword matching.

Instant Matching

Get personalized recommendations in seconds, not hours. Our system processes hundreds of grants to find your best matches.

Strategic Insights

Receive detailed explanations of why each grant is a good fit, plus strategic advice for strengthening your application.

Technology Stack

Grant Finder is built on cutting-edge cloud infrastructure to ensure reliability, scalability, and speed:

  • AWS Lambda: Serverless backend processing for instant scalability
  • Amazon Bedrock: Access to Claude 3 Haiku for advanced AI analysis
  • Grants.gov API: Real-time access to federal funding opportunities
  • Modern Web Technologies: Responsive design that works on any device

Parent Company

SelasBio

Grant Finder is proudly developed by SelasBio, a biotechnology company dedicated to advancing scientific research through innovative digital solutions. SelasBio specializes in creating tools that empower researchers, streamline workflows, and accelerate the path from discovery to application.

With deep expertise in both life sciences and technology, SelasBio understands the unique challenges facing today's researchers and is committed to building solutions that make a real difference in the scientific community.
